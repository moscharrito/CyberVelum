Our Story

Born From a Real Problem

In 2020, we watched SMEs across the UK get hit by wave after wave of cyber attacks — ransomware, phishing, data breaches — while enterprise-grade security remained out of reach. The tools existed. The expertise existed. But nobody was packaging it for the businesses that needed it most.

So we built CyberVelum. A managed security provider focused entirely on SMEs, powered entirely by the Microsoft security stack. No bloated toolsets. No unnecessary complexity. Just the protection your business needs, deployed and managed by experts who understand your world.

Today, we protect clients across the UK, keeping their people, devices, data, and reputation safe — so they can focus on what they do best.
