What It Is

Cloud-Native Security Information & Event Management

Microsoft Sentinel is a cloud-native SIEM (Security Information & Event Management) platform that collects security data from across your entire environment and uses AI-driven analytics to detect threats in real time.

Combined with built-in SOAR (Security Orchestration, Automation & Response) capabilities, Sentinel doesn't just detect threats — it responds to them automatically with pre-built playbooks, dramatically reducing response times.

Our expert SOC analysts monitor your environment around the clock, investigating every alert and responding to incidents in under 15 minutes. You get enterprise-grade security operations without building your own team.
