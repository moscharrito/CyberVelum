What It Is

AI-Powered Email Defence

Microsoft Defender for Office 365 is a cloud-based email filtering service that protects your organisation against advanced threats to email and collaboration tools — including phishing, business email compromise, and zero-day malware.

With Safe Links, Safe Attachments, and AI-driven anti-phishing policies, every email and file is inspected in real time. Malicious URLs are rewritten and detonated in sandbox environments. Suspicious attachments are analysed before delivery. Impersonation attempts are flagged and blocked automatically.

We configure and manage it all, so your team can focus on work — not on spotting scams.
