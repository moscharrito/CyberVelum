Compliance & Governance

Regulations like GDPR, Cyber Essentials, and ISO 27001 aren't optional — they're the cost of doing business. We make meeting them painless with Microsoft Purview, turning compliance from a burden into a competitive advantage.

GDPR Cyber Essentials ISO 27001
What It Is

Data Governance & Compliance Management

Microsoft Purview is a comprehensive data governance and compliance platform that helps you understand, manage, and protect your data across your entire organisation.

With Compliance Manager, you get a clear score showing exactly where you stand against GDPR, Cyber Essentials, and ISO 27001 — and step-by-step actions to close every gap. Sensitivity labels classify and protect your data automatically, while retention policies ensure you keep what you need and dispose of what you don't.

Audit logs give you a complete trail of every action taken on your data, so when auditors come knocking, you have the answers ready.

How It Works

From Gap Analysis to Audit-Ready in 30 Days

1

Compliance Gap Analysis

We assess your current compliance posture against GDPR, Cyber Essentials, and ISO 27001 — identifying exactly where the gaps are and what needs to be addressed.

2

Purview Deployment & Data Classification

We deploy Microsoft Purview across your environment and classify your data with sensitivity labels — so every document, email, and file is properly categorised and protected.

We configure DLP policies, retention rules, and information barriers tailored to your regulatory requirements — then train your staff so compliance becomes second nature.

Continuous monitoring of your compliance score, regular reports on policy effectiveness, and proactive adjustments to keep you ahead of evolving regulations.
