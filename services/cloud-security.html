Cloud Security

Your Microsoft 365 and Azure environments hold your most critical data. We secure every layer of your cloud infrastructure — from shadow IT discovery to real-time threat analytics — so you can embrace the cloud with confidence.

Cloud Protection Microsoft 365 Azure Security
What It Is

Complete Cloud Application Security

Microsoft Defender for Cloud Apps is a Cloud Access Security Broker (CASB) that gives you deep visibility and control over data travel across your cloud services — including Microsoft 365, Azure, and third-party SaaS applications.

It discovers shadow IT lurking in your environment, enforces Data Loss Prevention (DLP) policies across every cloud app, and provides real-time analytics to detect suspicious activity the moment it happens.

We configure and manage the entire solution so your cloud environment stays secure without adding complexity to your team's workload.

Business Outcomes

What This Means for Your Business

Cloud security isn't just about protecting data in transit. It's about knowing exactly what's happening across every cloud service your organisation uses.

Discover Shadow IT

Identify every unauthorised cloud application your employees are using and bring them under control before they become a risk.

Prevent Data Leaks with DLP

Enforce Data Loss Prevention policies that stop sensitive information from leaving your organisation through cloud apps, email, or file sharing.

Secure Third-Party SaaS

Govern and protect third-party SaaS integrations connected to your environment, ensuring no app becomes a backdoor for attackers.

How It Works

From Audit to Continuous Protection

1

Cloud Environment Audit

We map every cloud service, SaaS application, and data flow in your organisation to understand your full attack surface and identify shadow IT.

2

Defender for Cloud Apps Deployment

We deploy and configure Microsoft Defender for Cloud Apps as your CASB, connecting it to Microsoft 365, Azure, and your third-party SaaS applications.

3

DLP & Access Policies

We create and enforce Data Loss Prevention rules, conditional access policies, and session controls tailored to your business requirements.

4

Continuous Monitoring & Anomaly Detection

Real-time analytics detect unusual behaviour, impossible travel, and suspicious access patterns — with automated alerts and response to keep your cloud secure around the clock.
